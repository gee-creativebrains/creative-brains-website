# About Creative Brains

## Headline

The team that thinks and does.

## Story

Most agencies split strategy from creativity. Strategists write decks. Creatives execute. The handoff is where ideas die.

At Creative Brains, the people who think are the people who build. That's what makes the work sharper — and the process faster.

## Values

### Honest craft over slick sales
We don't overpromise. We show you what we can do, then we do it.

### Speed as a creative discipline
Fast doesn't mean rushed. Lean processes, clear thinking, no wasted time.

### Outcomes over aesthetics
Beautiful work that doesn't perform is just decoration.
